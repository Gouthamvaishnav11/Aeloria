Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Aeloria and our services.

What is Aeloria?

Aeloria is a cloud platform that allows you to deploy full-stack applications and static websites directly from GitHub to the World Wide Web with automated builds, testing, and deployments.

How much does Aeloria cost?

Aeloria offers a free tier for personal projects and small teams. We also have paid plans for larger teams and enterprises with additional features and support.

What technologies does Aeloria support?

Aeloria supports a wide range of technologies including React, Next.js, Vue.js, Angular, Node.js, Python, Django, Flask, Spring Boot, and many more. We're constantly adding support for new technologies.

How do I get started with Aeloria?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, connect your GitHub repository, configure your project settings, and deploy. Our platform will handle the rest with automated builds and deployments.
